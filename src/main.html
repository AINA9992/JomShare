QWERTY qwerty CREATE NOTES

1. Double click on this button "CREATE NOTES" to popup text area.

2. After the text area pop out, WRITE your notes on that area.

3. Then click "Save" button to display your notes.

4. Click "X" to exit the text area.

5. After that, the page will navigate to other page that store your notes.

6. Then, this message will appear YOUR NOTE IS SAVED at new page.

7. If you wish to "DELETE" your post, just "insert ID" beside your notes on.

8. Then, click DELETE button.

9. FYI, the new page will refresh after 15 second and back to home page.

10. Then your your post has "DELETED"

11. Finally you are DONE creating your notes.
